Recipe for "Risotto al Radicchio"

Ingredients for two/three people

  • Risotto Rice (Vialone Nano or Carnaroli);
  • Onions 500 gr.;
  • 1 Garlic Head;
  • 1l. Vegetable or Meat broth;
  • White cooking wine (500 cl.);
  • 1 teaspoon Nutmeg;
  • 30-50 gr. Butter;
  • 100 gr. Parmigiano Cheese.

Preparation

  1. Wash the rice thoroughly at least three times;
  2. Cut the onions and the garlic in tiny cubes;
  3. Prepare a pot with boiling broth on the side;
  4. Spread some oil on a frying pan and once it gets very warm, add the onions and the garlic and let the whole fry until it gets a golden texture;
  5. Add a spoonful of broth and let the onions and garlic cook for a couple minutes and then add the rice;
  6. Toast the rice for some time, until it starts to stick to the bottom of the frying pan;
  7. Add a glass full of white cooking wine and wait until it completely evaporates;
  8. Ad repetitiously spoonfuls of broth to the rice and continue stirring until the fluidity decreases. Keep the fire high and once it does, add broth again;
  9. Try and taste a grain of rice after 20-25 minutes of cooking and stirring and add the nutmeg;
  10. Once the rice is ready, take it out of the fire and let it rest for at least 5 minutes;
  11. Add the butter, the parmesan cheese and stir until both are completely melted;
  12. The rice is ready.
