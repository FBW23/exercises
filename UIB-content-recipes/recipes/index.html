Fried fish with garlic and mamaliga

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Fresh fish
  • Salt, Piper
  • Cornflour
  • Garlic - creamy Sarmuzac
  • Sunflower oil for fried
  • Mamaliga

Preparation

  1. Passed the fish through th cornflour
  2. Heat a larger amount of oil in a deeper pan,and there we put the fish.Whole fish are fried one at a time, the pan is not crowded! We work with special pliers and take care not to burn with the hot oil that splashes if we are not careful! Each fried fish is put out on a tray lined with paper towels to get rid of excess oil.
  3. Made Sarmuzac, meaning a thick garlic paste rubbing the garlic cloves in the mortar with a little coarse salt, oil and a little vinegar or lemon juice. It is not obligatory to put the sou
  4. Making Mamaliga. In the boiled wather we put salt, and Cornflour and mix until it will be the same consistent.The mălăliga can be hot poured into a tray and can be cut into different shapes for garlic and mango meats.

Several presentation options for these fried fish. You choose the shape you like best.
