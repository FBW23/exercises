Fun Facts about Live Aid

Crowd Size Matters:

Live Aid audience estimates are all over the map. History.com reports 110 countries watched the massive concert courtesy of 13 satellite feeds. The Guardian ratchets the number up to 150 countries.

In the Air Tonight

Phil Collins didn’t want to miss a moment of either Live Aid concert. So he played drums at Wembley Stadium early in the day before jetting to Philadelphia to drum for a reunited Led Zeppelin. More on that debacle in a moment.
